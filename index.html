About Us

Kanisa Digital exists to provide alternative software solutions to modern day Christians who are forced to rely on apps from the secular world which, while useful and beneficial, can become a channel for bad content or even cause Christians to sin. The Bible instructs us to guard our hearts and to cut off anything that leads us to sin, and it was with this in mind that Kanisa Digital was born.
